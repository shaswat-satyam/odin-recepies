Mango Candy Lemonade

Description

Summers are here and it's time to dive into some refreshing and soul satiating drink and what can be better than a chilled lemonade made with a punch of mango candies. No wonder, this drink will revive your childhood memories of indulging in some delicious mango candies along with the goodness of lemon. Follow us through some simple steps and enjoy this easy and refreshing drink made in just a few minutes.


    Ingredients
  • 3 tablespoons dry mango powder
  • 7 lemon slices
  • 6 Mint leaves
  • 750 ml Sprite
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes
  • 2 pinches Black salt
  • 4 spring mint
    Steps
  1. To make this easy drink you can either take sweetend mango powder or use candies. For this particular recipe we have used mango candies. To begin with take a mortar pestle or a grinder and make a fine powder of the mango candies.
  2. Take a large jar and add in lemon slices, mint leaves and muddle it well. Next, add in the mango candy powder and muddle it again with lime and mint.
  3. Add in crushed ice and pour sprite, add in rock salt, crushed mint leaves and enjoy!
